About Us

HowzBout Inc. is the first social media application that address those confusion clusters which have the potential to challenge anyone’s patience. HowzBout empowers individuals to easily manage more personalized ‘Choices’ and rapidly make better decisions on what to do, where to go and what to eat for their recreational time or that special social occasion for themselves or with friends.

Why use HowzBout?

FAST & EFFICIENT

HowzBout reduces the amount of time spent on trying to figure out what to do, where to go, and how to plan activities.

SMART & PERSONALIZED

HowzBout uses top-tier technology and algorithms to determine the best recommendations for users based on user generated information, demographics, behavior data, and more.

Cutting Edge

HowzBout supplies personalized recommendations unique to each individual user.

Data Driven & Precise

HowzBout's Data Management system allows businesses of all sizes to obtain immense data that will help them make the critical decisions regarding their sales, marketing, brand, and overall operations.
